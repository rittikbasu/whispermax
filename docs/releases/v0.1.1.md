## whispermax v0.1.1

i wanted a fast local dictation app i could trust, so i built whispermax.

- in-app update support with a small home update pill
- smarter insertion across more native apps, browsers, and editors
- more honest pasted vs copied feedback
- better clipboard behavior for fallback insertion
- safer recorder failure cleanup
- continued polish across onboarding, settings, and dictionary flows

notes:
- automatic update checks are now on by default every 6 hours
- the app is still not notarized, so macos may ask you to open it manually the first time
